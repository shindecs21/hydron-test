Gabadron-NT

Gabadron-NT

Pregabaline 75 mg, Nortriptyline Hydrochloride 10mg

Your masterstroke for

All types of neuropathy pain

Pregabalin 75mg
is Alpha 2 delta ligand. Decreases the pain by modulating calcium channel activity of the nerve cells.
Nortriptyline 10mg
is a tricyclic antidepressant. Increases the levels of serotonin and noradrenaline. Stop the movement of pain signals in the brain.
Effective In In Neuropathy Pain
  • Neuropathic Pain Associated with:
    • Spondylitis
    • Arthritis
    • Sciatica
  • Diabetic Neuropathy
  • Peripherial Neuropathy
  • Diabetic Neuropathy
